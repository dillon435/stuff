Basic Info.

The name of the video game is Halo 3 ODST, and was given an ESRB rating of "mature" because of the graphic dipiction of violence. Halo 3 ODST was released on September 22nd, in 2009, and takes between 7 to 9 hours to complete. It was published by Microsoft Game Studios, and was developed by Bungie. It won the VGX award for Best Original Score.
