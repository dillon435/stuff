Mr. C's Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces of unsweetened chocolate
  • ¾ cup of butter
  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of pure vanialla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 and ½ cups of chocolate chunks

Directions

  1. Microwave butter and chococlate until metled
  2. Stir until combined
  3. Add sugar and mix until well combined
  4. Add eggs and vanialla. Beat until mixed
  5. Stir in flour
  6. Stir in chocolate chunks
  7. Pour into greased pan
  8. Bake at 350° for 30 minutes
  9. Cool in pan
  10. Cut into squares

Variations
